👤 Customer FAQ
How do I book a service? ▼
Search for a provider, tap their profile, choose a service, pick a date and time, and confirm your booking. The provider has 15 minutes to accept. Once accepted you will be prompted to pay to secure your slot.
What happens if a provider does not show up? ▼
You are fully protected. If a provider no-shows you get a full automatic refund. No questions asked. Report the provider through the app so we can take action.
Can I cancel a booking? ▼
Yes. Go to My Bookings and tap Cancel on your booking. Cancellations made more than 2 hours before the appointment are fully refunded. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.
What is the late penalty? ▼
If you arrive more than 15 minutes late to your appointment a 50% late fee applies. This protects providers who have blocked out time for you.
How do I leave a review? ▼
After your service is marked as completed go to My Bookings and tap Leave Review. You can leave an anonymous review if you prefer.
Is my payment safe? ▼
Yes. All payments are processed by Paystack — Nigeria's most trusted payment provider. Preen never stores your card details. Your money is held securely and only released to the provider after your service is completed.
How do I block or report a provider? ▼
Go to the provider's profile, tap the ••• button in the tabs, and choose Report or Block. Reports are reviewed by our team within 24 hours.
💼 Provider FAQ
How do I get verified? ▼
Sign up, complete your profile, and upload at least one skill with a photo. Adding a skill video speeds up verification. Our team reviews and verifies you within 24 hours.
How long do I have to accept a booking? ▼
You have 15 minutes to accept or decline each booking request. After 15 minutes the booking auto-declines and the customer is notified. We recommend turning on WhatsApp notifications so you never miss a request.
How does payment work for providers? ▼
When a customer pays for a booking the money is held securely by Preen. After the service is completed the payment is released to your withdrawal account minus Preen's 10% platform fee. You can withdraw anytime your balance is above ₦1,000.
What is the Preen Score? ▼
Your Preen Score is calculated from your ratings, booking completion rate, response time and how long you have been on the platform. A higher score means better visibility in search results and the leaderboard.
Can I offer house calls? ▼
Yes. Enable house calls from your profile settings. Customers can request you come to their location. You set your travel fee and decide which requests to accept.
What happens if a customer is late? ▼
If a customer is more than 15 minutes late a 50% late fee is automatically charged to them. This protects your time. You can still choose to cancel the appointment if they are too late.
