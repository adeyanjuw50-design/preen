Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Preen. We are Nigeria's beauty booking platform connecting customers with skilled beauty professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how Preen Technologies Limited collects, uses and protects your personal information when you use our app and website. By using Preen you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Account information — name, email, phone and WhatsApp number
  • Booking information — services, dates, times and amounts
  • Location data — approximate location when you search for nearby providers
  • Provider portfolio — photos and videos of work, bio and service descriptions
  • Payment information — processed securely through Paystack. We never store card details
  • Reviews and ratings you leave for providers
  • Device type and operating system for app performance

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and manage your bookings
  • Send confirmations and reminders via WhatsApp and email
  • Connect customers with the right providers
  • Calculate provider Preen Scores
  • Process payments and provider withdrawals
  • Improve the platform and fix issues
  • Prevent fraud and keep the platform safe

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Location Data

Preen requests location access to show nearby providers. This is optional. Your precise location is not stored permanently. You can disable location access anytime in your phone settings.

Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Data is stored on Supabase servers with encryption at rest
  • Payments are processed by Paystack — a PCI-compliant provider. We never store card details
  • Database row level security ensures users only access their own data
  • Staff access is logged and restricted by role

WhatsApp Notifications

Preen uses WhatsApp to send booking confirmations, reminders and updates. By providing your WhatsApp number you consent to these messages. You can opt out anytime from your profile settings.

Sharing Your Information

  • Service providers — your name and contact details are shared with the provider you booked
  • Paystack — receives necessary information to process payments
  • Supabase — our database provider
  • Cloudinary — stores provider photos and videos

We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct — update your information from profile settings
  • Delete — close your account to permanently delete your data within 30 days
  • Withdraw consent — opt out of non-essential communications anytime

Children's Privacy

Preen is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us immediately if you believe a child has registered.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time and will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Last updated May 2026.

Questions about your privacy?

Contact us at privacy@preen.ng — we respond within 48 hours.
