Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account including your name, email address, phone number and location. We also collect booking information and payment details processed through our secure payment provider Paystack.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process bookings, send appointment reminders, improve our services and communicate important updates. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Location Data

We request access to your location to show you nearby service providers. You can disable location access at any time in your phone settings. Location data is not stored permanently on our servers.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption at rest and in transit. Payment information is processed by Paystack and never stored on Preen servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data at any time. You can close your account from your profile settings which will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Contact Us

For any privacy related questions contact us at privacy@preen.ng
